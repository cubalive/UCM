SCORING (implement a clear, explainable formula)
Compute per driver per window:
- Punctuality (0–40): % trips where pickup_actual <= pickup_scheduled + grace (default 10 min)
- Completion (0–25): completed / assigned
- Cancellations (0–15): penalty for cancel/no-show
- GPS quality (0–10): based on stale GPS rate (>5 min) and jitter rate
- Acceptance/Responsiveness (0–10): accepted within X minutes of assignment (default 2 min)

Clamp to 0..100. Store components JSON. Make thresholds configurable with env vars.

ANOMALY DETECTION (incremental + cheap)
Implement these initial anomaly rules (all tenant-scoped):
1) DRIVER_STALE_GPS: driver has no GPS update for >5 minutes while marked online or assigned active trip
2) DRIVER_LATE_SPIKE: driver late rate today is > (30d avg + 20%) and has >= N trips today
3) CLINIC_CANCEL_SPIKE: clinic cancellations today > (30d avg + threshold) and >= N
4) ETA_DEGRADE: p95 latency for ETA engine or directions calls exceeds threshold for last 5 minutes OR breaker trips > 0
5) QUOTA_NEAR_LIMIT: company at >= 85% of driver limit OR active trip limit

Each anomaly should:
- Upsert active anomaly row (update last_seen_at, details)
- Auto-resolve: mark is_active=false if not observed for 2 consecutive intervals
