CORE RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
---------------------------------------

1) LOGOUT drivers must:
- NEVER appear in assignable driver lists
- NEVER appear on live map
- Be shown only inside a separate section: "LOGGED OUT"
- Not counted as active drivers

2) ONLINE drivers = last_seen_at within 120 seconds AND dispatch_status != 'off'

3) OFFLINE / LOGOUT = 
   dispatch_status='off'
   OR last_seen_at older than 120 seconds

4) AVAILABLE FOR ASSIGN =
   ONLINE AND dispatch_status='available'

5) BUSY =
   ONLINE AND (dispatch_status='enroute' OR active trip in progress)

6) HOLD/BREAK =
   ONLINE AND dispatch_status='hold'

7) UNASSIGNED vehicle ≠ driver status.
Vehicle null is just a label.
