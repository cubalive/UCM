SECTION 1 — CLINIC OPS DASHBOARD (MAIN SCREEN)
-------------------------------------------------
Create a new page:
Clinic → Ops Dashboard

Top KPI CARDS (real-time, refresh 15s):

1. Patients En Route to Clinic
   count where direction=TO_CLINIC and active

2. Patients Leaving Clinic
   count where direction=FROM_CLINIC and active

3. Arrivals Next 60 Minutes
   count ETA < 60 and TO_CLINIC

4. Late Risk
   ETA > scheduled_time + threshold (10 min)

5. No Driver Assigned
   scheduled trips today with no driver

6. Completed Today
   completed trips today

7. No-Shows Today
   patient_no_show or driver_no_show

8. Recurring Patients Active
   dialysis recurring today
