FEATURE: PATIENT EMAIL FOR PRIVATE/INTERNAL + INVOICE DELIVERY (PAY LINK)
We already classify patients as internal vs private.
Private/internal patients must have an email so we can send invoices and payment links.
Clinic patients should NOT require email (optional), because clinic billing differs.

GOALS
1) Add patient.email field (stored in DB).
2) Validation rules:
   - PRIVATE patients: email REQUIRED
   - INTERNAL patients: email REQUIRED (if they receive invoice/pay link)
   - CLINIC patients: email OPTIONAL
3) When an invoice is created for a private/internal trip:
   - generate/send invoice email to patient with:
     - invoice summary
     - payment link (Stripe Checkout or hosted invoice link)
4) Store delivery status for audit (sent/failed/timestamp).
5) Do NOT expose secrets. Do NOT reset DB.

------------------------------------------------------------

- email_to text nullable

- email_sent_at timestamp nullable
- email_error text nullable (store last error)

All changes must be additive. No resets.

PHASE 5 — INVOICE + PAYMENT LINK GENERATION (PRIVATE/INTERNAL ONLY)
------------------------------------------------------------
When creating invoice for a trip:

1) Determine billing target:
- If trip belongs to clinic (clinic_id not null and not "UCM Direct/Private Pay") -> clinic billing flow (do NOT email patient unless explicitly enabled)
- If trip is private/internal -> patient billing flow (email required)

2) Ensure patient email exists:
- If missing -> block invoice creation and show:
  "Patient email required to send invoice/payment link."

3) Create payment link:
Preferred approach (Stripe):
- Use Stripe hosted invoice OR Checkout Session OR Payment Link
- Store stripe_invoice_id or stripe_checkout_session_id on invoice record

4) Send email to patient:
- Use existing email service (SendGrid/Postmark/Resend/etc). If none exists, implement minimal provider using ENV keys.
- Email includes:
  - Patient name
  - Trip date
  - Amount
  - Secure payment link
  - Company branding "United Care Mobility"

5) Update invoice delivery fields:
- email_to
- email_status
- email_sent_at
- email_error if failed
